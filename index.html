Always do your best. What you plant now, you will harvest later.

Plants are solar powered air purifiers whose filter never needs replacing.

Humans depend on plants for food, either directly or as feed for domestic animals. Agriculture deals with the production of food crops, and has played a key role in the history of world civilizations. All plants need space to grow, the right temperature, light, water, air, nutrients, and time.
Plants make up the backbone of earth's diverse landscape that provide hundreds of unique habitats necessary for life. Plants are unique in that they can continue to grow for the rest of their lives. The presence of meristems at specific sites throughout the plant's body gives it this ability.
